Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of digital services including mobile app development for iOS and Android, software development, e-commerce operations, brand planning and promotion, new media content creation, digital product sales, and mobile management solutions.

How can I download your apps?

Our mobile applications are available for download on both Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Simply search for our app name or visit our website for direct download links.

Do you offer custom development services?

Yes, we provide custom software and mobile application development services tailored to your specific business requirements. Please reach out with your project details for a consultation.

What industries do you serve?

We serve clients across various industries including retail, technology, healthcare, education, entertainment, and lifestyle. Our solutions are adaptable to meet diverse business needs.

What is your response time?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent technical issues, please use our support email with a detailed description of the problem.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide and have experience adapting our services to meet international standards and requirements. Contact us to discuss your global business needs.
